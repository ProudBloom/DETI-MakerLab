What is MakerLab?

MakerLab is a IoT laboratory for the students and professors among all the University of Aveiro.

It is located at the Department of Electronics, Telecommunication and Informatics.

Every student can rent any equipment from there. Each electronic device or even circuit parts can be requested.

Project & team

The aim of this project is to make the use of MakerLab easier. Thanks to the web application, managing, renting and browsing through the equipment of the lab will become much easier and convenient both for students and the technician who administrates the whole facitily.

To meet team members and the supervisor, please visit Our team section.
